Amendment to the Loan Agreement and Extension of Closing Date

Dear Mr. Janjughazyan,

We refer to the Loan Agreement (“Loan Agreement” between the Republic of Armenia
(“Borrower”) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (““Bank’’) dated November
18, 2015, as amended, for the Third Public Sector Modernization Project. We also refer to your letter dated
May 29, 2019, requesting a restructuring of the Project which requires certain amendments to the Loan
Agreement. Please note that unless the context otherwise requires, the capitalized terms used in this
Agreement has the meanings ascribed to them in the General Conditions or in the Loan Agreement.

In light of the above, we propose to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:

l. All references to the term “Project Implementation Department” is replaced with the term “Office
of the Prime Minister” in the entire Loan Agreement.

2. The acronym “PID” is replaced with the acronym “OPM” in the entire Loan Agreement.

a All references to the term “Civil Service Council” is replaced with the term “Civil Service Office”
in the entire Loan Agreement.

4. The acronym “CSC” is replaced with the acronym “CSO” in the entire Loan Agreement.
5. Section I. A.1(a) and (c) of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement are hereby amended to read as
follows:

“(a) for the purpose of ensuring the proper oversight, coordination and execution of the Project,
vest the overall responsibility for implementation of the Project in the PSRC which shall: (i) act as
the Project Steering Committee; (ii) be headed by the Borrower’s Deputy Prime Minister or Chief
of Staff, who is also the Project Director; and (iii) be responsible for providing strategic guidance
for the Project and approving the Project Operational Manual, Project annual budgets, and key
financial management and procurement documents.”

“(c) for the purpose of complying with the fiduciary aspects of the Project, vest in OPM the
responsibilities of financial management and procurement, and ensure that OPM, including PMG,
has adequate number of staff with terms of reference and qualifications satisfactory to the Bank to
fulfill its responsibilities with respect to Project implementation.”
Section IV. B.2 of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:

“2. The Closing Date is November 30, 2022.”

read as follows:

The following new paragraph is inserted in alphabetical order in the Appendix to the Loan Agreement:

“OPM” or “Office of the Prime Minister” means the Borrower’s office in charge of carrying out
fiduciary functions under the Project.”

Amendments to Section I of the Appendix to the Loan Agreement are as follows:

(a) Definition no. 6 is deleted entirely and amended to read as follows:
“0; “CSO” means the Borrower’s Civil Service Office, duly established and operating
pursuant to the requirements of the RA Law HO-205-N of March 23, 2018 "On Civil

Service" and Decision N 973 of July 17, 2018 of the RA Prime Minister “On approving
the Statute of the Civil Service Office of the Office of the RA Prime Minister.”

(b) Definition no. 8 is deleted entirely.
(c) Definition no. 10 is amended to read as follows:

“10. “GFMIS” or “Government Financial Management Information System” means the
Borrower’s system consisting of a series of automation solutions that enable the Borrower
to plan, execute and monitor its budgets.”

(d) Definition no. 13 is amended to read as follows:

“13. “Incremental Operating Costs” means the reasonable operational costs (which

would not have been incurred absent the Project) incurred by PMG related to Project

implementation, including, inter-alia: non-durable goods (such as, office supply, minor

Z
office equipment, vehicle and equipment maintenance, costs for fuel, literature, field trips,
meetings and in-country travel costs related to the Project (including accommodations,
transportation costs and per diem), PMG’s staff salaries (excluding civil servants’ salaries)
staff health insurance and social benefits, communication costs, reasonable banking
charges and media advertisement costs, and other expenses that may be agreed with the
Bank during the Project implementation.”

(e) Definition no. 20 is supplemented with words “, or any legal succession thereto” after the
words “May 2, 2002”.

10. The Performance Monitoring Indicators set out in the supplemental letter related to the Loan
Agreement dated November 18, 2015 are amended as reflected in the Attachment to this letter.

11. All other terms of the Loan Agreement will remain the same.
Please confirm your agreement on behalf of the Borrower with the above amendments by signing,
dating and returning to us one of the original copies of this Amendment. This letter shall become effective

as of the date of countersignature.

Very truly yours,

INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
